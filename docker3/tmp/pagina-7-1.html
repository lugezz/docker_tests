Description

Position Overview:

The Project Coordinator will be responsible for all administrative support for entire business unit.  The candidate will be required to coordinate project-related compliance and reporting activities, such as identifying opportunities, preparing proposals, compiling field data collection in reports to customers, tracking and managing ongoing business performance metrics, maintaining and adjusting project schedules, coordinating team members and equipment, analyzing the weekly revenue and cost data, and change management.  This role will also interface and need to build solid relationships with select customers, subcontractors, and vendors

Essential Functions:

  • Prepare, maintain, and manage resource and cost loaded schedules in MS Project, Salesforce, and others as required
  • Assist in Managing customer systems
  • Assist with new customer account contractor requirements
  • Review field daily work reports and track performed quantities
  • Review weekly margin reports for cost and revenue performance
  • Collect data and produce analysis of revenue, cost, manpower and labor hours
  • Analyze project scope adherence during execution and determine variances and impact
  • Facilitate change order submittals per contract requirements
  • Manage the change order process and status
  • Assist in managing vendors, project material and material inventory as needed
  • Assist with execution and compliance with prime contractor contracts
  • Track and review invoices
  • Review and manage project costs for accuracy, ensure cost transfers and ensure corrections when needed
  • Manage and maintain accurate project schedules, estimates for labor, materials, and other direct costs
  • Accurately report project performance, revenue, cost, accruals, and forecasts, as required by company and customer
  • Provide input on potential areas for improvement in process management and operational efficiencies whenever possible
  • Travel when necessary to complete projects, with on-site presence throughout duration of projects
  • Work closely with operations to ensure projects are completed within budget and on time
  • Schedule appointments and maintain calendars
  • Schedule and coordinate staff and other meetings
  • Collect and distribute mail
  • Assist in preparing communications, such as memos, emails, invoices, reports, and other correspondence
  • Write and edit documents from letters to reports and instructional documents
  • Create and maintain filing systems, both electronic and physical
  • Work with finance and accounting to perform bookkeeping
  • Participate in project design meetings and propose to operations as needed
  • Evaluate customer feedback and develop solutions of potential problems, roadblocks, and technical hitches
  • Plan and manage team goals, project schedules and new information
  • Works with operations leaders on projects and coordinate all team members to keep workflow on track
  • Manage project-related paperwork by ensuring all necessary materials are current, properly filed and stored in an easily accessible location
  • Direct project correspondences by preparing and reviewing project proposals, impact reports, memos, meeting minutes and emails
  • Works with operations leaders and clients to identify and define project requirements, scope, and objectives
  • Adhere to budget by monitoring expenses and implementing cost-saving measures
  • Monitors Salesforce to identify and coordinate business opportunities
  • Maintains all sustainability tracking
  • Direct line of support to customers
  • Follow up on customer leads
  • Support organizing and executing industry event presence
  • Order materials and equipment as operations leaders deem necessary
  • Help identify valuable industry events
Qualifications:

Education and Experience:

  • High school diploma required, Business degree or equivalent preferred
  • 2+ years of Business or Project Management related experience
  • Experience in the Oil and Natural Gas Industry
  • Organizational skills, detail-oriented, problem-solving skills, work under strict deadlines and exhibit a team player attitude
  • Knowledge of generally accepted accounting principles relating to payables and sales tax accounting
  • Supervises staff and coordinates activities with other groups to ensure accurate and timely completion of activities and problem resolution
  • Proficient in Microsoft Office, accounts payable and purchasing Intermediate Excel skill is desirable. Must be familiar with Pivot tables and V-Lookup
  • Experience with accounts payable and purchasing
  • Excellent interpersonal, oral, and written communication skills
