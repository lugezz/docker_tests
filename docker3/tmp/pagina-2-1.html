Crew Lead Apprentice

Location: Portland, OR
Type: Full Time
Minimum Experience: Mid Level

High Hourly Wage! Cash Tips Daily! Fun Engaging Culture!

Do you want an active work environment?

Do you want to test & develop your leadership skills?

How about meeting and helping new people each day?

Do you like to work with supportive and friendly coworkers?

If you answered yes, we would love to talk to you! We are looking for enthusiastic, hardworking people who are excited about learning new skills, providing exceptional customer service, and being part of a growing company!

RESPONSIBILITIES

  • Lead a team in the relocation of clients’ households
  • Manage moving projects from start to finish of varying size
  • Develop a plan for the customer, assign and communicate tasks to the crew, and lead by example.
  • Provide a commitment to making the safety of our client’s belongings your #1 priority
  • Adapt to obstacles and solve problems while balancing your safety, and the time and needs of our clients
  • Ensure the welfare, safety, and success of your mover crew
  • Using creative techniques and tools to reduce physical strain
  • Consistently providing exceptional Customer Service and ensuring communication with clients and team

REQUIREMENTS

  • Exceptional customer service skills and communication
  • Dependable, professional, and solution oriented
  • Strong leadership skills
  • Clean criminal background & driving record (3 years)
  • Minimum age of 21 to be considered for commercial driving role
    • No CDL Required
  • Able to regularly lift 75 lbs without assistance, stand, bend, stoop and climb stairs
  • Able to commute daily to NW Portland, OR
  • Ability to travel inter-state for long distance moving projects

COMPENSATION

  • $18.50 - 21/ Hour Starting Wage
  • $200 per week cash tips on average
  • Performance bonuses, promotions, and overtime
  • Opportunities for training, growth & advancement
  • Complimentary gym on site
  • Full time employees are eligible for medical, dental, and vision benefits
  • Summer kickball team and company events

OPPORTUNITIES

  • Independently lead moves across the nation, seeing new places and meeting new people along the way
  • Lead Movers in the execution of various job types and sizes, in various locations
  • Stay active & healthy, or join our recovery and fitness program if you need a boost.
  • Receive driver training to drive commercial vehicles
  • Develop and improve your leadership & customer service skills
  • Create lifelong relationships & memories
